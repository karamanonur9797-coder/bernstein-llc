# Brand Copy – BERNSTEIN LLC.

> Source: `Prompts/03_CONVERSION_COPY_ARCHITECT.md`

## Brand Voice

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Tone | Authoritative, warm, quietly confident |
| Target | Urban creatives and professionals, 25–40, global, value craft over logos |
| Primary objective | Conversion |

### Voice Rules
- **Never use:** "luxury", "premium", "exclusive" (show, don't tell)
- **Always use:** sensory language, material references, process and craft cues
- **Max headline length:** 6 words
- **Subheadlines:** ~15 words

## Page Copy

### Homepage
- **H1:** "Craft That Carries Its Weight."
- **Sub:** "BERNSTEIN LLC. – Ready-to-wear and footwear built for the way you move through the world."
- **CTA:** "Explore the Collection"

### Collections Page
- **H2:** "What We Make"
- **Sub:** "Each piece designed with obsessive attention to material, cut, and longevity."

### Product Page
- **Material callout:** "Full-grain leather. Hand-finished edges. Built to last a decade."
- **Size guide CTA:** "Find Your Fit"
- **Add to cart:** "Add to Bag"
- **Trust line:** "Free shipping worldwide. 30-day returns. No questions asked."

### About Page
- **H1:** "Made With Reason."
- **Story:** BERNSTEIN LLC. is built on a simple belief – that well-made things should be accessible to people who care about them. Based everywhere. Made with intent.
